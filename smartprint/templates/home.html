How Inkzo Works

Simple automated printing process in just a few steps

1. Upload Files

Upload single or multiple files with individual or global print settings. Supports PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG and more formats.

2. Pay Securely

Complete payment through our secure payment gateway. Multiple payment options available for your convenience.

3. Auto Print

Files are automatically sent to vendor and printed immediately. Real-time processing ensures quick turnaround.

4. Collect Prints

Receive completion notification with token number. Visit vendor location and collect prints using your token.
